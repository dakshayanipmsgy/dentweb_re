Why EnergyCare matters

A rooftop solar plant is expected to work for decades. Without periodic checks, small issues like dust, loose wiring, communication failure, earthing weakness or inverter errors can quietly reduce savings.

  • Better customer confidence after installation
  • Faster fault identification and service planning
  • Opportunity to upgrade to hybrid battery or EV charging later
